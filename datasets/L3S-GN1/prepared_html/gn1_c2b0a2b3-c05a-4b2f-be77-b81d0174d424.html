30 December 2007
Port Vale 1 Luton 2
Coca Cola FOOTBALL LEAGUE ONE

Matthew Spring's stoppagetime free-kick ruined Joe Anyon's 21st birthday as Luton left Vale at the foot of the table.

Spring struck his 35-yard shot well, but Vale keeper Anyon looked in control until the ball sped inside his left-hand post.

Luton got off to a terrific start with centre-back Jaroslaw Fojut firing them ahead.

Darren Currie's right-wing free-kick found its way to the far post where the Polish youth international struck an unstoppable half-volley past Anyon.

Luton threatened a second when Anyon saved David Edwards' header from point-blank range, but Vale got the tonic they desperately needed in firsthalf stoppage time, when Callum Willock steered his shot home.

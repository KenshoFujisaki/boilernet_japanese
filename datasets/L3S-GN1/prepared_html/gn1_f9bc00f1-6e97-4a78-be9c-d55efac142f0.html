    iTunes does movie deal with Fox

    Malaysia Sun

    Friday 28th December, 2007



    Twentieth Century Fox and Apple say they will do a deal which allows movie rentals through iTunes.



    iTunes is Apple?s online music store.



    Movie rentals would allow iTunes users to view each individual movie for a limited time before automatic expiry.



    The Fox arrangement will be different to other movie studio deals done by Apple, which limit users to older movies.

OAKLAND, Calif., Dec. 29 Two free throws by Marcus Camby with nine seconds left provided the final margin Friday in the Denver Nuggets' 124-120 shootout win over Golden State.



The teams combined for 189 field goal attempts -- 40 from three-point range. They will meet again Sunday in Denver.



Denver had a nine-point lead in the first half, but the game remained close throughout the final two quarters.



The Nuggets took the lead for good with 8:54 remaining on two free throws by Allen Iverson that made it 106-104. Golden State was still within two, however, when Camby was fouled by Al Harrington with nine seconds to go. His two free throws gave him 15 points for the night.



Iverson scored 39 and Carmelo Anthony added 30 for the Nuggets.



Golden State was led by Stephen Jackson with 32.

Gunners star to comeback

20080109 15:24
Arsene Wenger is likely to go with a young side for tonight's Carling Cup clash with Spurs but Robin van Persie is set to return.
The Dutch striker has not played since October because of injuries to both his leg and knee but he is expected to be back tonight although Lassana Diarra, who is wanted by Newcastle and Portsmouth, looks likely to miss out.
And there will be no place for Jens Lehmann in goal with Lukasz Fabianski playing behind a defence that will include Johan Djourou and Philippe Senderos.
Wenger is looking for his youngsters to go one better than last season when they were runners up in the competition to Chelsea.
He said: "Of course you want to win trophies especially when you have a very young side which has already been in two finals.

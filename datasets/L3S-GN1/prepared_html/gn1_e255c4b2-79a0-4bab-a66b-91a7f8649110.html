Two of three embassies in Canberra have been cleared of danger after suspicious package incidents.

A third package, at the British High Commission, was still being investigated.

An ACT police spokesman said white powder found in packages at the Israeli and United States embassies had been classed as non-suspicious after checks.

He said such incidents occurred from time to time in Canberra and it was standard procedure to have them checked.

Reports: Taiwan opposition surges to big lead in legislative elections

TAIPEI, Taiwan (AP) - Taiwan's main opposition Nationalist Party surged to a big lead in early vote counting in the island's legislative elections Saturday, TV reports said.

Local TV stations said that with voting for 49 of the 113 seats counted, the Nationalists had won 42 seats and the ruling Democratic Progressive Party had triumphed in 7.

The Nationalists and their allies enjoy a slight majority in the current 219-seat Legislature.

Cop kicked in head as hundreds brawl in bar

Article from: AAP
  • Font size: Decrease Increase
  • Email article: Email
  • Print article: Print

January 08, 2008 04:50am

NINE people were arrested overnight as police had to call in back-up to disperse a central Sydney bar brawl involving up to 500 people.

The fight broke out among patrons attending a function at Pontoon Bar about 1.25am (AEDT) today and, at its peak, it is alleged a police officer was kicked in the head.

"The fight was ... at a nightclub in Darling Harbour," a NSW police spokeswoman said today.

"Police requested further assistance to disperse a group of up to 500 people.

"One police officer received minor bruising after allegedly being kicked in the head."

Police arrested seven men and two women at the scene.

The spokeswoman said they would be charged with offences ranging from being armed with intent to commit indictable offence, hindering police, assaulting police and affray.

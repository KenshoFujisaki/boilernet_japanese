Aussies need doubles win at Hopman Cup

Posted January 1, 2008 16:10:00

  • Map: Perth 6000

Australia's team in the Hopman Cup tennis tournament in Perth will need to win their mixed doubles match tonight to remain in contention for the final on Friday night.

Alicia Molik was beaten in her women's singles tie against India's Sania Mirza 2-6, 6-2, 4-6 earlier today.

Her partner Peter Luczak beat Rohan Bopanna in the men's singles match, 7-6, 6-3.

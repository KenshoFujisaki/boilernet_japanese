T.J. Ford Cleared To Practice
| Jan 09 2008 07:48 PM PST By JHOHL27
Topics: tj nba ford raptors
T.J. Ford has been cleared to practice, that is practice not game but that means he is getting close to coming back.



Article:



Raptors guard T.J. Ford was cleared to resume workouts Wednesday after visiting a spine specialist in Cleveland.



It isn't immediately clear as to when he will start working out again or appear in a game for the Raptors, but things are steadily moving in that direction.

Dowling set for absence

2007-12-30 14:49:00.0
Munster star Ian Dowling is set to spend a spell on the sidelines after sustaining a knee injury.
The winger could face up to eight weeks out with the problem that he picked up against Ulster during the week.
He had to be stretchered off after 76 minutes at Musgrave Park and is now set to sit out the Heineken Cup games against Clermont Auvergne and London Wasps.
